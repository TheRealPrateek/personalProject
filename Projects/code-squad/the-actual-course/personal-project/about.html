What is Guit-archive?

Guit-archive is a free tablature web-site consisting of user-made tabs. Unlike other websites we could mention, Guit-archive is and always will be free to use. No subscriptions, no apps, no nonsense - just guitar tabs.

Who writes the tabs?

You do! All of our tabs are user-generated. Just create a profile and start uploading tabs from our tab editor.
